  ABOUT SOULVEST


HELPING YOU INVEST IN PASSIONATE IDEAS

SoulVest is an online investment platform that provides the general public an opportunity to put their money to work in a meaningful way. We go beyond the basic ESG screens used in overly diversified mutual funds and create focused portfolios that are specifically designed and managed by our in-house team of investment experts. Companies in our portfolios are required to be “best in class”, creating the most positive impact within areas such as climate change, diversity, human rights, and animal welfare, while also minimizing companies profiting from tobacco, weapons, or gambling.

Our goal is empower people to invest for good. We believe that there is a shift in investment philosophy, and that investing in a socially responsible way is the wave of the future. We want to effect change by providing a vehicle that is accessible to everyone which pools money from the masses to create positive shifts in the free market economy.
