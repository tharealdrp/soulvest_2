  PORTFOLIOS


Socially responsible investing is no longer something people do just to feel good. It’s now where the money will be made.

Expect a return on your investment (ROI) while knowing you are affecting change in a meaningful way. Use SOULVEST as a primary investment strategy or simply a way to create positive social impact.

HOW WE INVEST

We go beyond the basic ESG (environment, social, and governance) screens used in overly diversified mutual funds and create focused portfolios that are specifically designed and managed by our in-house team of investment experts. Companies in our portfolios are required to be “best in class”, creating the most positive impact within their sectors.


WHAT ARE YOU MOST PASSIONATE ABOUT?

Choose from 1 or BOTH of the portfolio segments below and we will create a personalized impact portfolio focused on your goals.


ENVIRONMENT

companies directly involved in positively effecting change with a focus on the following: climate change, hazardous waste, waste management, energy, sustainability (recycling, agricultural practices, water management, etc.)

SOCIAL

companies directly involved in the positively effecting change with a focus on the following: diversity, human rights, consumer protection, animal welfare


TRANSPARENCY

We want you to know about the companies you are investing in, and provide detailed information on all companies we invest in, and reasoning behind their selection in our process.
